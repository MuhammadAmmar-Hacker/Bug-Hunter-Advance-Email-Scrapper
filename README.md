## Features

- **Multi-Mode Scraping** — Scan the current page, auto-scrape across multiple Google search result pages, or scan all open tabs at once.
- **Domain & Deep Scan Modes** — Choose a fast main-page-only scan, or a thorough crawl through contact, about, and security pages.
- **Auto Background Crawl** — Passively collects emails while you browse, with no manual action required.
- **Smart Categorization** — Automatically flags high-value bug bounty emails and detects paid/reward programs.
- **Categorized Google Dorks** — Pre-built dork categories (FinTech, SaaS, Healthcare, Fresh VDPs, Paid Programs, and more), plus a Smart Dork Builder with selectable Year, Currency, and Country.
- **Smart Blacklist** — Automatically filters out your own Gmail address, social media domains, and open bounty platforms such as HackerOne, Bugcrowd, and Intigriti. Any domain can be blocked with one click.
- **Approach Templates** — Save custom outreach email templates and send them directly through Gmail compose.
- **Live Progress Tracking** — Real-time statistics, with pause, resume, and stop controls during scraping.
- **Multiple Themes** — Green Neon, Red Neon, and Black & White.

### Option 2: Load as a Chrome Extension

1. Download or clone this repository.
2. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Enable **Developer mode** using the toggle in the top-right corner.
4. Click **Load unpacked**.
5. Select the cloned/downloaded `Bug-Hunter-Advance-Email-Scrapper` folder.
6. The Bug Hunter icon will appear in your Chrome toolbar.

## How to Use

1. Click the **Bug Hunter** icon in your Chrome toolbar to open the extension popup.
2. Go to the **Search** tab and choose a scraping mode:
   - **Current Page** — scrapes emails from the page you are currently viewing.
   - **Google Multi-Page** — runs a Google search and auto-scrapes results across multiple pages.
   - **All Open Tabs** — scrapes emails from every tab currently open in your browser.
3. Select a scan depth: **Domain** (fast, main page only) or **Deep** (crawls contact/about/security pages too).
4. Press **Start** and monitor live progress, including emails found, domains scanned, and queue status.
5. Use the **Dorks** tab to launch pre-built or custom Google dorks targeting specific industries, or use the Smart Dork Builder to filter by year, currency, and country.
6. Use the **Blacklist** tab to exclude your own email, social media domains, or any platform you do not want included in results.
7. Click any discovered email to save a custom outreach template and send it directly through Gmail.
8. Toggle **Auto Background Crawl** to let the extension continue collecting emails passively as you browse.
